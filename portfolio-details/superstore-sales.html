SuperStore Sales Analysis Dashboard

The SuperStore Sales Analysis project presents a comprehensive dashboard that showcases sales data from 2014 to 2017, highlighting key metrics such as total transactions, total sales, total discounts, and profit percentages. It provides insights into top-selling states and product categories, enabling better business decision-making and strategic planning.
